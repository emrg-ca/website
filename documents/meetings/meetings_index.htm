2005-02-26
 On Feb 26, EMRG hosted the Ottawa District ARES meeting at the OFS training Centre. This meeting brought the management teams from neighbouring ARES groups that make up the Ottawa District as well as ARES groups that border on the Ottawa District. The main purpose of the meeting was to understand each other better and to start work on a mutual aid capability.
