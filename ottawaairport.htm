The Ottawa Airport is controlled by the MacDonald-Cartier International Airport Authority. In a major Airport emergency or disaster, the Emergency Measures Radio Group (EMRG) would typically provide communications for organizations supporting the relief effort in the community, not radio operators at the airport. Volunteer radio operators would deploy to support the City of Ottawa Community Services and the Ottawa Red Cross with shelters, backup communications for the Hospitals if required, or as additional support for logistics such as food services for agencies at the Airport.

The Ottawa Airport Fire Chief has been an important promoter of EMRG within the Emergency Response community. In May 2002, EMRG participated in the Airport Exercise Integration which involved all aspects of Emergency Response in Ottawa, including People Services, Red Cross and the Hospitals.
