Volunteer Emergency Radio Communications -Serving Ottawa Canada

Amateur radio volunteer emergency radio communications in Ottawa is continuing to gain benefits from the ever growing relationships within the City. EMRG works with the following agencies and organizations in Ottawa;

    • City of Ottawa; Emergency Measures Unit, Fire, EMS, Police, People Services
    • Ottawa Hospitals
    • Ottawa Red Cross
    • Ottawa Airport


Check out the EMRG accomplishments for 2002. EMRG was a partner in many events with City of Ottawa, Provincial and Federal agencies. Like all partnerships, you have to put effort in to get results out.
