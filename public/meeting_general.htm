Notice of General Meeting

Sunday, October 14, 2012 

*** EXERCISE *** EXERCISE *** EXERCISE ***

EMRG will hold a simulated emergency test (SET) exercise this Sunday,
October 14, 2012.

All members and interested persons are requested to check in to our
SET net between  09:00 and 10:00 on 146.880-  (136.5Hz tone) for
further instructions.  This exercise is in three parts, you can excuse
yourself anytime during the exercise if you have other commitments.

If you haven't updated or confirmed your membership information
sometime in the last year, you are requested to send a confirmation
email to Margaret (membership@emrg.ca) with updates as appropriate.
Please see http://emrg.ca/EMRG-504_Membership_Form.pdf for required
information.
