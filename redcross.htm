The Ottawa branch of the Canadian Red Cross and the Emergency Measures Radio Group have a signed Memorandum Of Understanding (MOU). Through this agreement, the Red Cross will have access to the communications capabilities of EMRG. EMRG operates and maintains the radio equipment located at the Red Cross offices on Catherine Street.

Radio Amateurs of Canada (RAC) and the National Red Cross have a high level MOU in which RAC will provide communications capabilities to the Red Cross, through the local ARES group.
