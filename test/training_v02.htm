Welcome to the EMRG Training Page! The purpose of these pages is to provide EMRG members with a central point of contact to training documents, links and references that are of interest to Amateur Radio Operators with a focus on Emergency and Disaster communications.

These pages will be continuously updated with current information on training being offered as well as links to training material available on the Internet.
