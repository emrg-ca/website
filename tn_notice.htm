Tour Nortel

Sunday May 6, 2007

On Sunday May 6th 2007, the Emergency Measures Radio Group (EMRG) will be providing communications for the Tour Nortel 2007.

The "Tour" is a fundraiser for the Children's Hospital of Eastern Ontario (CHEO).

This is a morning event that should finish early in the afternoon.

This is the same event that the Ottawa Amateur Radio Club (OARC) has provided communications for in the past.

This year EMRG was asked to assist and we have agreed to provide communcations as both a public service and as a communications exercise.
